Who We Are

A Community of Friends is a non-profit organization committed to ending homelessness through the provision of quality permanent supportive housing and supportive services. We believe that everyone deserves a place to call home and the support they need to live healthy and fulfilling lives.

What We Do

Our after-school programs offer a variety of activities and support services designed to meet the unique needs of children facing homelessness and mental illness. From academic support and mentorship to recreational activities and therapeutic interventions, we strive to empower children to reach their full potential.

How You Can Help

Join us as a volunteer and make a difference in the lives of children in need. Whether you can spare a few hours a week or want to get involved in a specific project, there are plenty of opportunities to get involved.

Connect with us on social media to stay updated on our latest news, events, and initiatives.
