Our Organization

We are a team of dedicated individuals committed to enhancing after-school programs for children facing homelessness and mental illness. Our work involves analyzing data from various sources using tools such as Joint Application Development (JAD) sessions and surveys with a funnel structure. These methods allow us to gather insights and develop strategies to improve the programs provided by A Community of Friends (ACOF), a nonprofit organization focused on providing comprehensive support to individuals experiencing chronic homelessness and mental illness.

Our Mission

Our mission is to break the cycle of trauma for children affected by homelessness and mental illness by providing them with opportunities for growth, education, and stability. We believe that every child deserves a safe and nurturing environment where they can thrive academically, emotionally, and socially.

Our Vision

We envision a future where all children, regardless of their circumstances, have access to high-quality after-school programs that support their holistic development. Through our efforts, we aim to empower children to overcome adversity and become successful, resilient members of society.

Our Programs

At our organization, we offer a range of after-school programs designed to meet the diverse needs of children facing homelessness and mental illness.

  • Homework Assistance: We provide tutoring and academic support to help children succeed in school.
  • Therapeutic Enrichment Activities: Our programs include art therapy, music therapy, and other activities to promote emotional well-being.
  • Reintegration Support: We assist children in transitioning back into school and community life after experiencing homelessness.

Our Impact

Through our programs, we have seen significant positive changes in the lives of the children we serve.

  • Improved academic performance and engagement in school
  • Enhanced social and emotional well-being
  • Increased resilience and confidence
